What to Expect

You can expect to have an adventure full of fun and safety with one of our highly trained and certified rafting guides. We begin each route with a safety training where we ensure that everyone knows what's going on and feels safe doing this. Throughout the rafting ride, your instructor will give loud and clear directions along the way. In case of emergencies, all our rafting guides are CPR certified and have a walkie talkie on them at all times.

Expect to be thrown about and tossed around. White water rafting is a high intensity activity that will put a good amount of strain on your body. You can expect to be sore the next day, which subsides after 2 days in most cases.
