Content

Home page

Our facility is located conveniently 5 miles east of Rexburg, ID and 36 miles north from the GHHJA Headquarters in Idaho Falls, ID. We have provided professional training in Show Jumping, Dressage, and Cross Country for over 15 years and are dedicated to bring out the best rider in everyone. Alongside our professional training, we provide a safe and enjoyable atmosphere for riders and horses in our newly renovated arenas and stables.

Facility

Spring Hill Equestrian is nestled on approximately 60 acres of land offering both pasture board and stall board. The facility is open seven days a week from 6am to 10pm with a spacious heated indoor arena and 2 smaller outdoor arenas for its boarders and lesson use. A beautiful heated viewing room with many amenities, including leather sofas, is available to relax after riding or to use while watching the lessons. The facility is monitored by full-time caretakers who live on the property and a security system with both day and night vision cameras. Riding trails and cross country courses are located around and on the property, giving riders options outside the arena.

Our Staff

Brynn Mulano is the owner of Spring Hill Equestrian and has been riding her whole life. Brynn began showing in three day eventing when she was 10 years old and won many junior division shows and championships. Brynn studied Animal Science at Texas A&M while she continued to show accross the country. After completion of her degree, Brynn spent 7 years riding for Team USA internationally, where she brought them 9 first place awards in three day eventing. Upon leaving Team USA, Brynn went through the American Riding Instructor Certification Program (ARICP) to become a certified riding instructor. 15 years ago, Brynn picked up home in College Station, TX and moved out to Rexburg, ID where she bought land and built Spring Hill Equestrian. Brynn has trained many of her clients to win championship titles both nationally and internationally. Brynn has recieved numerous awards for her training effenciency, especially in the dressage discipline.

Pablo Escobar has been working along side Brynn Mulano since their time in college. Pablo has been riding since the age of 9 and knew from a young age he wanted to devout his life to it. Pablo competed from a young age and excelled in placing in his divisions. Though Pablo has competed in various events, he prefers and specializes in show jumping. Pablo is ARICP certified in Huntseat, Eventing, and Stable Management. Pablo began working for Spring Hill Equestrian in 2011 and plans to spend a very long time here.

Lauren Boam has been riding since she was 11 and quickly climbed the ranks. Lauren loves eventing and specializes in cross country. Though Lauren is only 26 years old, she has recieved many awards and championship titles. Lauren is still competing, but has also recieved her ARICP certificate to train in Eventing, Huntseat, and Dressage. Lauren recently completed a Master's degree in business management and is going to school to recieve a PhD in business analytics.
